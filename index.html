THE UPCOMING OLYMPICS

The world's greatest sporting event is coming to the City of Love. The Olympic Games are set to take place in Paris, France from July 26th to August 11th 2024. This will be the third time that Paris has hosted the Olympic Games, having previously done so in 1900 and 1924.

The 2024 Olympics will feature 32 sports and 329 events, including new additions like surfing, skateboarding, and breaking. Many fan-favorite events like athletics, swimming, and gymnastics will also return.

The Paris 2024 Organizing Committee has placed a strong emphasis on sustainability, with a goal of hosting the first Olympic and Paralympic Games with a positive impact on the environment, aiming to benefit Parisians for years to come, ensuring the Games have a transformative impact on the local community.
